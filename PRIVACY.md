# Privacy Policy for YzPzCode

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## Introduction

YzPzCode is an open-source desktop application for managing multiple AI coding agent CLIs. This privacy policy explains how our software handles your information.

## Data Collection

**YzPzCode does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

### What stays on your computer:
- Workspace configurations
- CLI settings and preferences
- Terminal session history
- Application settings

All data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

## Third-Party Services

YzPzCode interacts with the following AI CLI tools that you configure:
- Claude (Anthropic)
- Codex (OpenAI)
- Gemini (Google)
- Opencode
- Cursor

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data when you use their CLI tools. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies:
- [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Analytics

YzPzCode does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry features.

## Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this file on our GitHub repository.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/wolfenazz/YzPzCode/issues).
